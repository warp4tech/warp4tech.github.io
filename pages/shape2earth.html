Google Maps and Google Earth provide the most comprehensive and up to data mapping platform on the market.

Shape2Earth provides the tools you need to use Google's mapping data for your own use.

  • Shape2Earth for MapWindow

    View and prepare your shapefiles in the free and open source MapWindow GIS.

    Export the shapefiles into KML to view in Google Earth Desktop, or deploy your KML online with Google Maps.

  • Shape2Earth Engine

    A very simple yet powerful tool for converting shapefiles to KML, Shape2Earth Engine turns Google Earth Desktop into a light weight GIS.

    Quickly and easily view data trends and prepare visual products to convey spatial information to you and your customers

  • Image2Earth

    Gain access to then best and most up to date satellite imagery from Google at the click of a button

    Grab the Google Imagery you need to see the world as it actually is in your GIS software.
